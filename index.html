Who Are We?

The Bahamian Developers Association was formed to foster an environment for technology enthusiasts and business persons alike. Following that pursuit, the Nassau Technology annual conference came to fruition. Despite 2021 being our inaugural edition, there will definitely be more to come.

The primary purpose of the conference this year is to gather individuals from various sectors to create a larger and more integrated community, whilst also facilitating connections, learning new ideas, and investing in said ideas.

Our topic this year is “The state of technology in The Bahamas“ and our speakers will provide prospectives from their respective industries.

During the conference, we will:

  • Discuss challenges and outline strategies and ideas that will aid in the advancements of our nation.
  • Connect entrepreneurs, innovators, investors, service providers, tech enthusiasts, key decision makers, and others of similar nature.
  • Provide a platform to discuss trends in telecommunications, digital identity, 5G, artificial intelligence, and automation.
