About The 2021 Nassau Technology Conference.

The Bahamian Developers Association was formed to foster an environment for technology enthusiasts and business persons alike. To that end the Nassau Technology annual conference came to life, 2021 is our first year with many more to come!

The primary purpose of this year's conference is to bring together individuals from many different sectors and create a larger community, while facilitating connections, learning of new ideas, and investments in those ideas.

Our topic this year is “The state of technology in The Bahamas“ and our speakers will provide prospectives from their respective industries.

The purpose of this year's conference is:

  • To outline strategies and ideas to advance our nation.
  • To connect entrepreneurs, innovators, investors, service providers, tech enthusiasts, and key decision makers.
  • Provide a platform to discuss trends in Telecomms, Digital Identity, 5G, Artificial Intelligence, and Automation, while also providing a platform to discuss green field opportunities and challenges we face as a nation.
